Heatwave Safety Tips

Before a Heatwave

  • Install window reflectors
  • Weather-strip doors/windows
  • Identify cooling centers
  • Prepare emergency supplies

During a Heatwave

  • Stay hydrated
  • Limit outdoor activities
  • Wear lightweight clothing
  • Check on vulnerable neighbors

Recognizing Heat Illness

  • Heat cramps: muscle pains
  • Heat exhaustion: heavy sweating, weakness
  • Heat stroke: high body temperature, confusion
  • Seek medical help immediately for severe symptoms

Emergency Contacts

  • Local Emergency: 911
  • Heat Helpline: 1-800-HEAT-99
  • NOAA Heat Safety
